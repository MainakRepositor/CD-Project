About Us

The WhatsApp Chat Analyzer tool will analyze and collect a number of stats like most active user, most busy time period, and total and average messages per user in case of group chats.

  • Reveal unknown facts and surprise your friends with insights into your communication.
  • Easy and interactive visualization which are easy to understand and fun look at.
  • No data is ever transfered to the server. All computing is done locally in your browser.

Have you ever wondered who is the most talkative person in your group chat? Who starts the most conversations? What are the most common words used? What are the most frequently used emojis? Get Answers to all these questions.
